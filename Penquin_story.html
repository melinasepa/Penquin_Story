A Story of Drama and Deceit

There was once a penquin who was hungry. He searched far and wide for food.

He came upon a dog with a backpack on. The penquin said, "Have thouest any food kindly dog?"
The dog said, "Yes, but you must give me an egg."

The penquin said, "I'm a guy."
The dog said, "I know the male penquin carries the eggs."
Penquin said, "Fine."
The penquin took out his eggs and "accidentally" made them fall towards a bunch of rocks.



Then he went and collected them and handed the dog an egg. The dog handed the penquin some carrots and said, "thanks for the egg." The penguin said, "no problem" then quickly took off. Later the dog tried to eat the egg and realized it was a rock.


THE END
